How is this different from a traditional property manager?

Traditional managers juggle dozens of properties with limited staff. Our AI systems handle routine tasks instantly — pricing, guest messages, maintenance detection — while our team focuses on complex problems and quality oversight. Better service, lower cost.

What if I already have a property manager?

We handle the transition. Most property management agreements have a 30-day notice period. We'll coordinate the handover so there's zero gap in service — your property stays listed and earning throughout.

Is there a lock-in contract?

No. We offer flexible agreements because we believe our results speak for themselves. If you're not happy, you can leave — but our properties consistently outperform, so most owners stay.
