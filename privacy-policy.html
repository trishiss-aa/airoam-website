We reserve the right, at our discretion, to modify or remove portions of this Privacy Policy from time to time and amendments will be effective immediately upon publishing on the website. We recognise the importance of protecting the privacy of information collected about visitors to our website and users of our services, in particular information that is capable of identifying an individual ("personal information") and are committed to complying with the Australian Privacy Principles under the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth). This Privacy Policy governs the manner in which your personal information, obtained through the website and our services, will be dealt with. We welcome your comments and feedback.

Personal information

Personal information about visitors to our site and users of our services is collected only when knowingly and voluntarily submitted. For example, we may need to collect such information to provide you with further services or to answer or forward any requests or enquiries. We may not be able to answer your enquiries at an acceptable standard if you do not identify yourself. It is our intention that this policy will protect your personal information from being dealt with in any way that is inconsistent with the applicable Australian Privacy Principles and other applicable laws in Australia.

Use of information

Personal information that visitors and users submit to our site or services is used only for the purpose for which it is submitted or for such other secondary purposes that are related to the primary purpose, unless we disclose other uses in this Privacy Policy or at the time of collection.

Purposes for which we may collect personal information include, but are not limited to:

  • Being able to sell and promote our products and services to you
  • To provide you with products and services that you have requested from us
  • To communicate with you, including about products and services and special offers which might interest you
  • To provide your personal information to third parties that assist us in providing products and services you have requested
  • To improve and train our AI systems and services in order to enhance user experience (where permitted by law and in accordance with this Privacy Policy)
  • To personalise your experience through AI-driven recommendations, insights, and content
  • To comply with laws or regulations or to comply with any directions given by regulators or authorities

Copies of correspondence sent from the website, that may contain personal information, are stored as archives for record-keeping and back-up purposes only.

AI data collection and usage

Airoam utilises artificial intelligence technologies as part of its service offerings. This section outlines how your data may be collected, used, and processed in connection with our AI-powered features:

  • Data inputs: When you interact with Airoam's AI-powered tools (such as chatbots, virtual assistants, automated booking systems, or recommendation engines), we may collect the content of your interactions, including text inputs, queries, preferences, and feedback.
  • AI processing: Your data may be processed by AI systems to generate responses, recommendations, pricing information, and other outputs. This processing is carried out to provide and improve our services.
  • AI training and improvement: Anonymised and aggregated data derived from user interactions may be used to train and improve our AI models and algorithms. We take reasonable steps to de-identify personal information before it is used for training purposes.
  • Automated profiling: Airoam may use AI to create profiles based on your usage patterns, preferences, and interactions to provide personalised services. You may opt out of automated profiling by contacting us.
  • Third-party AI processors: We may engage third-party AI service providers to process data on our behalf. These providers are bound by contractual obligations to protect your personal information and are only permitted to process your data in accordance with our instructions and applicable Australian privacy laws.
  • Data retention for AI: Data collected through AI interactions is retained only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes for which it was collected, or as required by law. You may request deletion of your AI interaction data by contacting us.

We strive to ensure the security, integrity and privacy of personal information submitted to our sites and services, and we continually review and update our security measures in light of current technologies. However, we will endeavour to take all reasonable steps to protect the personal information you may transmit to us or that is processed through our AI systems. Once we do receive your transmission, we will also make our best efforts to ensure its security on our systems.

Our employees and the contractors who provide services related to our information systems are obliged to respect the confidentiality of any personal information held by us. However, we will not be held responsible for events arising from unauthorised access to your personal information.

AI-specific security measures

In addition to our general security practices, we implement the following measures in relation to our AI services:

  • Encryption of data transmitted to and from AI systems
  • Access controls to limit who can access AI-processed data within our organisation
  • Regular audits and assessments of our AI systems and third-party AI providers to ensure compliance with applicable privacy standards
  • Data minimisation practices to ensure only necessary data is processed by AI systems
  • Incident response procedures specific to AI-related data breaches or security events

Collecting information from users

  • IP addresses: Our web servers gather your IP address to assist with the diagnosis of problems or support issues with our services. Information is gathered in aggregate only and cannot be traced to an individual user.
  • Cookies and applets: We use cookies to provide you with a better experience. These cookies allow us to increase your security by storing your session ID and are a way of monitoring single user access. This aggregate, non-personal information is collated and provided to us to assist in analysing the usage of the site. Users have the ability to accept or decline cookies by adjusting the web browser settings.
  • AI interaction data: When you use our AI-powered features, we may collect metadata about your interactions, such as timestamps, session duration, and the nature of your queries. This data is used to improve AI performance and user experience.
